Teriyaki Chicken

This recipe is easy to cook and ideal for students who are learning how to cook. It only takes 20 mins to make. Lets get started!

Teriyaki Cicken

Ingredients

These are the ingredients that we are going to need.

  • 2tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 6 skinless and boneless cicken thighs, sliced
  • 2 large garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 thumb-sized piece ginger, grated
  • 50g runny honey
  • 30ml light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, to serve
  • 4 spring onions, shredded, to serve
  • sticky rice, to serve
  • steamed bok choi or spring greens, to serve

Method

Follow these two simple steps.

  1. Heat the oil.

    2. Heat the oil in a non-sticky pan oven a medium heat. Add the chicken and fry for 7 min. Add the garlic and ginger and fry for 2 mins. Stir the honey,, soy sauce, vinegar and 100ml of water. Bring to the boil and cook ultil the chicken is sticky and coated.

  2. The last touch

    3. Scatter over the srping onions and sesame seeds, then serve the chicken with the rice and steamed veg.
