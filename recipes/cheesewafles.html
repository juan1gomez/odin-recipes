Cheese Waffles

Make cheese waffles in under 15 min. with this recipe

Ingredients

These are the ingredients that we are going to need.

  • 1tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 3/4 c. shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 3/4 c. shredded cheddar

Method

Follow these two simple steps.

  1. Prepare the mixture

    2. Combine mayonnaise and butter in a bowl. Spread the mayonnaise mixture onto the top side of each slice and flip two of them. TOp each bread with a slice of American cheese, half Monterrey jack cheese and half cheddar cheese. Sandwich with the remaining slice of bread.

  2. Heat it

    3. Heat a waffle iron over medium heat. Add one sandwich to the waffle iron and close it. Cook it until cheese melts.
