Spanish Breakfast

Make a Spanish Breakfast in under 15 min. with this recipe

Ingredients

These are the ingredients that we are going to need.

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Olive oil
  • Crushed tomato
  • Salt

Method

Follow these two simple steps.

  1. Toast the bread

    2. Put bread on the toaster until golden

  2. Add tomatoes

    3. Add the tomatoes and spread them on the toast.

  3. Add a dash of olive oil

    4. A dash of olive oil will give it a special flavour.

  4. Add salt to your liking
